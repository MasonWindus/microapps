Scoring Rules

Each player picks 5 golfers (duplicates OK). Standings show avg stroke score and avg position across each player's 5 golfers. Missed cuts and withdrawals: the golfer gets the highest round score posted by any golfer that round, and for position they get the worst surviving position + 1. Picks are saved in your browser.
